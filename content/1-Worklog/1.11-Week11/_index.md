## Work Summary

This week focused on completing every remaining feature of the application before the final delivery. I reviewed the shopping process, product management module, and administrator functions to ensure they worked correctly.

Extensive testing was performed to identify user interface issues, incorrect business logic, and unexpected system behaviors. Any detected bugs were fixed immediately, and the application was retested to confirm the solutions.

I also optimized application performance by reducing unnecessary requests, improving page loading speed, and optimizing image resources. AWS services such as EC2, RDS, and Amazon S3 were reviewed to ensure they were operating reliably after deployment.

Finally, I prepared the technical documentation, including the AWS architecture diagram, database schema, deployment guide, and installation instructions.
